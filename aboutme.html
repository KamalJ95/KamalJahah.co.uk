Welcome to KamalJ

Hello and welcome to my website. I am Kamal Jahah, an undergraduate Computer Science student currently on placement at Eli Lilly and Company.

At Lilly, my main job role consists of being a Systems Analyst. Aside from my main job role, i am teaching myself front end development (as you are seeing here!). I enjoy front end development a lot more than back end development, since you can immediately see the work that you are doing. Also aside from this, I am learning the Atlassian products, including Jira, Confluence and Fisheye+Crucible. These collaboration tools are some of the most important tools in the DevOps toolchain, which i believe is the current and is the future.

I am also trying to learn Microsoft Powerapps and Microsoft Flow, for rapid application delivery and automation respectfully. I also believe that Microsoft Powerapps are the future, especially for business people who may not be as technical. Microsoft Powerapps allows for people to very quickly spin up and deliver applications, especially when they are only for internal use. On the other side is Microsoft flows. Microsoft flows is good for automating processes that should no longer need to be done manually. You can simply set triggers so when new items are added into a sharepoint, you can notify people that this has happened. Simple.
